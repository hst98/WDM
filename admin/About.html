About

URM application is aimed to serve as a platform to connect academic institutions, URM (underrepresented minority) candidates, and DEI (diversity, equity, and inclusion) officers.

We assist the academic institutions by providing them a list of potential URM candidates for PhD studies, postdoc positions and faculty positions, while also guiding the URM candidates by offering them a list of academic institutions for their postdoc or faculty applications.
